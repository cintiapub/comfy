More About Comfy Conf.

COMFY is a Conference APP that includes a lot of features, making easy and confortable the attendent experience on the events.

It was lauched in 2016, in San Francisco, California. The plataform is open source, helping a lot of people and companies around the world, engaging, connecting and providing all the informations needed for the conferences.

Tiago Garcia is the creator of this amazing and free App. He is the CEO and also the Software engineer. You can help COMFY and Tiago to improve our APP.
Download it now, COMFY Conf. is available for IOS and Android and it supports premium events.

Whether you’re hosting a conference, a festival, or events, having a great mobile event app can make a big impact for your brand. COMFY has a clean and organized design, providing a wonderful user experience for your audience. COMFY APP is easy and joy to use.

Be comfortable to enjoy your conference, you give everything in your hands.
